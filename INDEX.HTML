Grandma's Secret Apple Pie 🍏🥧

A Generational Delight Passed Down With Love 💖

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight. It combines the perfect balance of sweetness and spice, topped with a golden, flaky crust. Perfect for family gatherings, holidays, or just because! 🥰

Ingredients 🍎

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced
  • Apple Ascorbic Acid
  • Pastry Con
  • Salt
  • Sugar (Sugar Water)
  • Modified Corn Starch
  • Cinnamon
  • Potassium Sorbate

Instructions 🍽️

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture, making sure each slice is coated.
  4. Roll out the pastry and place it in a pie dish. Fill it with the apple mixture.
  5. Top with the second layer of pastry and crimp the edges to seal.
  6. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  8. Let the pie cool before serving.

Tips for the Perfect Pie 🥧

  • Use a mix of tart and sweet apples for the best flavor balance (e.g., Granny Smith and Honeycrisp).
  • Make sure your butter is cold when making the crust for a flaky texture.
  • For a golden finish, brush the top of the pie with an egg wash before baking.
  • Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for extra indulgence! 🍨
